About Los Angeles time

Los Angeles follows Pacific Time, shifting between Pacific Standard Time (PST, UTC-8) and Pacific Daylight Time (PDT, UTC-7) depending on the season, in line with daylight saving rules observed in the United States and Canada.

Frequently asked questions

What time zone is Los Angeles in?

Los Angeles observes Pacific Time, which is Pacific Standard Time (PST, UTC-8) part of the year and Pacific Daylight Time (PDT, UTC-7) the rest of the year.

Does Los Angeles observe daylight saving time?

Yes. United States moves clocks forward one hour for Pacific Daylight Time (PDT, UTC-7) and back for Pacific Standard Time (PST, UTC-8).

How many hours difference between London and Los Angeles?

London is typically eight hours ahead of Los Angeles, shifting to seven or eight hours depending on the daylight saving schedules of the UK and US.
