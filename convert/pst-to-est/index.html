PST to EST time difference

Eastern Time runs 3 hours ahead of Pacific Time year-round, since both zones observe daylight saving on the same schedule within the United States, keeping the gap constant.

Frequently asked questions

What is the time difference between PST and EST?

Eastern Time is consistently 3 hours ahead of Pacific Time, because both zones shift for daylight saving on the same dates within the United States.

Does the 3-hour gap ever change?

Briefly, yes — for about one to two hours during the actual transition weekends in spring and autumn when one zone has changed and the other hasn't yet, but the steady-state difference is always 3 hours.

What is a good meeting time between Los Angeles and New York?

Late morning to early afternoon Pacific Time (10 AM-1 PM PT) overlaps with early-to-mid afternoon Eastern Time (1-4 PM ET), making it a practical window for most business calls.
