About ZoneSync

ZoneSync started from a simple frustration: most time zone tools either show too little information at a glance, or bury the answer behind ads and clutter before you can find the one number you actually need — what time is it there, right now.

We built ZoneSync to be the fastest way to answer that question, whether you're scheduling a call across continents, checking in with family abroad, or just curious what time it is on the other side of the world.

🎯

Accuracy first

Every conversion uses standard IANA time zone data, so daylight saving and historical changes are always handled correctly.

⚡

Built for speed

No sign-ups, no app downloads. Open the page and the time you need is already there.

🌍

Free, always

ZoneSync is free to use for everyone, supported by minimal, non-intrusive advertising.

How it works

ZoneSync runs entirely in your browser. When you load a page, your device's clock and the official time zone database built into modern browsers calculate every displayed time live — there's no server delay and no stale cached data. This is also why the site works offline once loaded and respects your privacy: we don't need to know who you are to tell you what time it is in Tokyo.

Who this is for

ZoneSync is used by remote teams coordinating meetings across continents, students and families staying in touch internationally, travelers planning itineraries, and developers who just need a quick, reliable time zone reference without digging through documentation.
